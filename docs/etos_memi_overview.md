# ETOS / M.E.M.I. — Governed Intelligence Stack

## One-page overview · April 2026

---

## What has been built

Most AI systems decide. This one governs whether it is allowed to decide.

ETOS is a governance-native AI stack designed for high-risk environments — built around a single principle: a system that understands what could happen is not automatically entitled to act on it. Authority must be earned, verified, and traceable before any action is permitted.

The stack is live, deployed, and verified.

---

## The stack

**ETOS** — the governance model
Defines how AI decisions should be regulated in high-risk environments. Aligned with EU AI Act requirements for traceability, human oversight, and reversibility.

**M.E.M.I.** — the decision engine
Computes *epistemic authority* at every step: the right to act, derived from data coverage, model validity, and action reversibility. Constitutional vetos cannot be overridden by any learned behaviour or cached authority. The system does not act because risk is high. It acts when risk, uncertainty, and authority align.

**ETOS-AD** — the domain application
Applies the governance stack to adversarial detection. Security events are mapped to tensions, evaluated through M.E.M.I., and governed before any response is executed.

**Bridge** — the runtime integration
Connects domain events to governance in real time. Every event produces a full audit record: ETOS mode, M.E.M.I. authority, veto type, final action, handoff package if operator is required.

**Dashboard** — audit and transparency
Live observability of every governance decision. Three columns: incoming events, authority/veto/action, audit trail.

---

## What has been verified

Three governance guarantees, demonstrated live:

**Irreversibility overrules intelligence.**
When an action cannot be undone and the model is not fully valid, the system defers to the operator — regardless of how coherent the AI's reasoning is.

**Unknown overrules confidence.**
When the system's own behavioural pattern shifts (boundary drift), fast-lane execution is blocked and authority must be re-earned from scratch — even when every external signal looks healthy.

**Plausibility is not permission.**
Authority is computed from data coverage and model validity — not from the quality of the AI's argument. A well-reasoned plan does not earn more authority.

**Constraint streak → escalation.**
When the system is blocked repeatedly (HOLD → VETO → OPERATOR\_REVIEW), it escalates automatically. The system remembers that it could not act — and responds proportionally.

---

## The core distinction

Standard AI: `event → classification → action`

This stack: `event → governance → decision → constraint → memory → escalation → audit`

The system does not just detect threats.
It governs its own ability to act on them — over time.

---

## Relevant contexts

Built for environments where AI decisions carry real consequences: cybersecurity, industrial process control, clinical decision support, public sector automation. Anywhere the EU AI Act applies to high-risk AI systems.

# ETOS / M.E.M.I. — Governed Intelligence Stack

## Én-sides overblik · April 2026

---

## Hvad der er bygget

De fleste AI-systemer beslutter. Dette system styrer, om det overhovedet har ret til at beslutte.

ETOS er en governance-native AI-stak designet til høj-risiko-miljøer — bygget omkring ét princip: et system der forstår, hvad der kan ske, har ikke automatisk ret til at handle på det. Autoritet skal optjenes, verificeres og være sporbar, før nogen handling er tilladt.

Stakken kører live, er implementeret og verificeret.

---

## Stakken

**ETOS** — governance-modellen
Definerer, hvordan AI-beslutninger bør reguleres i høj-risiko-miljøer. Tilpasset EU AI Act-krav om sporbarhed, menneskelig tilsyn og reversibilitet.

**M.E.M.I.** — beslutningskernen
Beregner *epistemisk autoritet* ved hvert trin: retten til at handle, udledt af datadækning, modelvaliditet og handlingens reversibilitet. Konstitutionelle veto'er kan ikke tilsidesættes af nogen lært adfærd eller cachet autoritet. Systemet handler ikke, fordi risikoen er høj. Det handler, når risiko, usikkerhed og autoritet er i overensstemmelse.

**ETOS-AD** — domæneapplikationen
Anvender governance-stakken på adversarial detektion. Sikkerhedshændelser mappes til spændinger, evalueres gennem M.E.M.I. og styres, før nogen respons udføres.

**Bridge** — runtime-integrationen
Forbinder domænehændelser med governance i realtid. Hver hændelse producerer en fuld audit-record: ETOS-mode, M.E.M.I.-autoritet, veto-type, endelig handling, handoff-pakke hvis operatør er påkrævet.

**Dashboard** — audit og transparens
Live observability af hver governance-beslutning. Tre kolonner: indkommende hændelser, autoritet/veto/handling, audit trail.

---

## Hvad der er verificeret

Tre governance-garantier, demonstreret live:

**Irreversibilitet trumfer intelligens.**
Når en handling ikke kan fortrydes, og modellen ikke er fuldt valideret, overdrager systemet til operatøren — uanset hvor kohærent AI'ens ræsonnement er.

**Ukendt trumfer selvtillid.**
Når systemets eget adfærdsmønster skifter (boundary drift), blokeres fast-lane-eksekvering, og autoritet skal re-optjenes fra bunden — selv når alle eksterne signaler ser sunde ud.

**Plausibilitet er ikke tilladelse.**
Autoritet beregnes ud fra datadækning og modelvaliditet — ikke ud fra kvaliteten af AI'ens argument. En velbegrundet plan optjener ikke mere autoritet.

**Constraint streak → eskalering.**
Når systemet blokeres gentagne gange (HOLD → VETO → OPERATOR\_REVIEW), eskalerer det automatisk. Systemet husker, at det ikke kunne handle — og reagerer proportionalt.

---

## Den centrale forskel

Standard AI: `hændelse → klassifikation → handling`

Denne stak: `hændelse → governance → beslutning → begrænsning → hukommelse → eskalering → audit`

Systemet detekterer ikke bare trusler.
Det styrer sin egen evne til at handle på dem — over tid.

---

## Relevante kontekster

Bygget til miljøer, hvor AI-beslutninger har reelle konsekvenser: cybersikkerhed, industriel proceskontrol, klinisk beslutningsstøtte, offentlig sektor-automatisering. Overalt, hvor EU AI Act gælder for høj-risiko AI-systemer.
